The Studio

9th Wonder operates as a working tattoo studio and cultural space. The environment is designed to support focus, cleanliness, and professional independence.

The studio is structured as a booth-rent space. Artists maintain full control over their work, schedules, and creative direction while sharing a disciplined, health-compliant environment.

This model keeps overhead low and standards high. It allows artists to work intentionally, without pressure to overproduce or dilute their voice.

9th Wonder supports collaboration without hierarchy and community without interference.

Workflow & Standards

  • Disposable-only tattooing
  • Health-compliant procedures
  • Clean, minimalist workstations
  • Artist-owned equipment and furniture
