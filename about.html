About 9th Wonder

9th Wonder is a working tattoo and art studio built around peace, professionalism, and respect for the creative process.

Tattooing is the foundation of 9th Wonder. We take walk-ins, book appointments, and work every day. The studio operates as a professional environment where craft, discipline, and consistency come first.

We believe peace is essential to meaningful work. Peace does not mean silence or restriction — it means creating a space where focus is protected and creative thought is uninterrupted.

Beyond tattooing, 9th Wonder exists as an environment for making. Artists are welcome to come create, experiment, and build — whether through drawing, painting, design, writing, or technical exploration.

We honor tattooing as a craft and a field of study. This includes not only application, but the mechanics, materials, and experimentation behind the tools themselves.

  • Professional working environment
  • Respect for process and pace
  • Support for collaborators as people
  • Intentional community engagement
  • Peace as shared responsibility
  • Craft over spectacle
