A modern tattoo studio and cultural space built on intuition, artistry, and community.

An Homage

9th Wonder is a working tattoo and art studio built to protect the act of creation.

We believe great work requires peace — not silence, not spectacle, but the mental space to focus, learn, and build with intention. Tattooing is our foundation: a craft rooted in discipline, repetition, and respect for both body and tools. We take walk-ins, book appointments, and work every day.

Those who work with us are collaborators. We invest in them as artists and as people, and we hold ourselves to a standard of professionalism that protects the space and the work within it.

Beyond the tattoo chair, 9th Wonder exists as an environment for making. Artists are welcome to come create — to draw, paint, design, write, build, and experiment. Our studio supports not only finished work, but the process behind it.

We support local artists through display, sales, and curated events, and we host community gatherings with intention — from family-friendly art nights to focused cultural meetups.

9th Wonder is not a destination. It is an environment.
